my top 3 movies of all time

Cinema Paradiso

Cinema Paradiso is an italian film directed by Giuseppe tornatore, the film is more like a love letter to cinema.

Hey Ram

Hey Ram is an indian film directed by Kamal Hassan, it is one of the greatest indian film of all time , the film blends history and fiction in a beautiful way and the music by Ilayaraja is one of the best you can experience.

My Neighbor Totoro

My Neighbor Totoro dircted by hayoa miyazaki is a kind of film which reminds us that "hope is good thing".
